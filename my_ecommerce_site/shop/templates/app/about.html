About Neel Dairy

Welcome to Neel Dairy, a proud Mumbai-based dairy that brings you the freshest and finest dairy products straight from our farms to your table. We are committed to quality and sustainability, ensuring that every product we offer meets the highest standards of excellence.

Our Mission

At Neel Dairy, our mission is simple: to provide nutritious and delicious dairy products that you can trust. We are dedicated to maintaining the integrity of our products through ethical farming practices and cutting-edge technology. Our goal is to enhance the well-being of our community by delivering the purest dairy products with unwavering commitment to quality and sustainability.

Established in the heart of Mumbai, Neel Dairy has been serving the community with pure and wholesome dairy products for over a decade. Our journey started with a simple mission: to provide nutritious and delicious dairy products that you can trust. We believe in maintaining the integrity of our products through ethical farming practices and cutting-edge technology.

At Neel Dairy, we are passionate about our cows and their well-being. Our farms are designed to provide a healthy and comfortable environment for our cows, ensuring they produce the best quality milk. We take pride in our modern processing facilities where hygiene and safety are our top priorities.

Our product range includes fresh milk, yogurt, cheese, butter, and more, all made from the highest quality milk. Whether you are looking for daily essentials or specialty items, Neel Dairy has something for everyone.

Join us on our journey to bring you closer to nature and experience the true taste of purity with Neel Dairy. Thank you for choosing us as your trusted dairy partner.
